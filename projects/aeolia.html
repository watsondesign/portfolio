ÆOLIA

Diagnosing the mechanical problems of airplanes is very time-consuming. Mountains of paper manuals make it difficult to identify and diagnose the problems. Not having a centralized digital diagnostic tool requires mechanics to rely on experience and intuition rather than data. Delayed flights result in upset passengers and sometimes costly cancellations for airlines.

ÆOLIA [ee-oh-lee-uh] is an app that provides predictive maintenance insights for airline mechanics by gathering and displaying historical data of airplanes. It uses artificial intelligence to find trends of failure and informs mechanics which planes and parts need to be inspected.
