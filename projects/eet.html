EET

Consumers and businesses trash billions of pounds of food every year. NRDC’s 2012 waste report revealed that Americans trashed up to 40 percent of their food supply—equivalent to $165 billion.

EET, short for Estimated Expiration Timer, is an app designed to track food purchases from local grocery stores and display optimized recipes when food is about to expire.
