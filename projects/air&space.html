air&space

The Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum collects, preserves, studies, and exhibits artifacts and archival materials related to aviation and spaceflight. Its research and outreach activities serve all audiences within and beyond its walls. The Museum commemorates the past and is committed to educating and inspiring people to foster an appreciation for human flight and exploration. Today, the museum maintains the world’s largest and most significant collection of aviation history.
